# Cacti — Social Impact & Shared Value

## Overview
**Cacti** is the venture within the **CodeLoop Ecosystem** dedicated to social impact and the management of shared value initiatives. It represents the "Social Fund" of the ecosystem, focusing on projects that benefit the community and promote inclusive development.

## Mission
To channel ecosystem resources into projects that bridge the gap between economic growth and social sustainability, ensuring that the advancement of technology also supports the human collective.

## Key Objectives
- **Social Fund Management:** Overseeing the allocation of capital to high-impact social projects.
- **Impact Integration:** Aligning social goals with the commercial success of the ventures in the constellation.
- **Community Empowerment:** Supporting initiatives that provide tools and knowledge to underserved communities.
