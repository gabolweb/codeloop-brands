# Cosmos — The Hyper Operating System

## Overview
**Cosmos** is the **Hyper Operating System Architecture** (HyperOS) that serves as the supreme orchestrator of the **CodeLoop Ecosystem**. It is the digital firmament upon which all other sub-operating systems and ventures are built and interconnected.

## Mission
To provide a unified, scalable, and resilient architectural framework that seamlessly integrates work, finance, and communication into a single digital reality.

## Structural Position
Cosmos sits at the top of the ecosystem stack, managing the interoperability between:
- **Mainland:** The Work OS.
- **Bastion:** The Finance OS.
- **Remotica:** The Communication OS.
- **mainOS:** The core kernel shared by all entities.

## Core Capabilities
- **Orchestration:** Seamlessly moving data and context between different planetary ventures.
- **Interoperability:** Ensuring that a "Looper" (professional) has a unified identity and experience across all ecosystem nodes.
- **Evolutionary Design:** Allowing the ecosystem to expand by adding new "planets" (ventures) without breaking the core system integrity.
