# GVP — Global Venture Park

## Overview
**GVP (Global Venture Park)** is the operational and physical infrastructure backbone of the **CodeLoop Ecosystem**. It provides the high-performance "industrial parks" and support systems needed for ventures to scale efficiently.

## Mission
To provide the physical and operational "fertile ground" where ecosystem ventures can grow, offering state-of-the-art facilities and centralized support services that eliminate the overhead of scaling.

## Key Components
- **Industrial Parks:** Advanced hubs for manufacturing, warehousing, and Research & Development.
- **Operational Support:** Centralized IT services, legal support, and cross-functional workflow management via the DSS department.
- **Venture Incubation:** Active support for new ecosystem entities, providing them with the "Venture Park" infrastructure from day one.

## Strategic Value
GVP ensures that ecosystem ventures don't have to build their own operational infrastructure from scratch, allowing them to focus entirely on their core innovation and market growth.
