# ILG — International Labor Gateway

## Overview
**ILG (International Labor Gateway)** is a specialized global platform operating within the **CodeLoop Ecosystem** (often in partnership with the DMCC in Dubai) focused on the mobilization and management of international human capital.

## Mission
To modernize global labor relations by providing a secure, compliant, and Web3-enabled gateway for international hiring, payment, and mobilization of remote professionals.

## Key Features
- **Global Hiring:** Enabling companies (primarily in Dubai/DMCC) to hire contractors from regions like Brazil, Angola, and the Philippines with full legal compliance.
- **Dedicated Visa Programs:** Facilitating specialized visa pathways (such as 3-month yearly stays) for international contractors.
- **Web3 Labor Management:** Utilizing the ecosystem's blockchain infrastructure (Bastion) to ensure transparent and instant cross-border payments.

## Strategic Role
ILG serves as the bridge between international talent and global enterprise demands, reducing the friction of hiring across different legal and financial jurisdictions.
