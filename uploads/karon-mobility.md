# Karon Mobility — Intelligent Movement

## Overview
**Karon Mobility** is the urban logistics and intelligent movement arm of the ecosystem. It provides the software and operational layer for efficient transportation, focusing on smarter city movement rather than just more infrastructure.

## Key Products
- **FleetOS:** Core fleet management platform.
- **RouteMesh:** AI-powered routing engine that adapts to live traffic and constraints.
- **LastMile:** End-to-end delivery infrastructure for urban logistics.

## Strategic Value
Karon Mobility ensures that the physical movement of goods and people within the ecosystem's global nodes is optimized for speed, cost, and environmental impact.
