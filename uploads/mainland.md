# Mainland — The Productivity Hub

## Overview
**Mainland** is the primary Operating System within the **CodeLoop Ecosystem** dedicated to work, productivity, and the execution of the "Dynamic Work" framework. It serves as the interface between professionals and the project structures they inhabit.

## Mission
To provide a seamless, augmented environment where human intelligence is supported by artificial tools, allowing for maximum efficiency in remote and decentralized work environments.

## Hierarchy & Position
In the **COSMOS HyperOS** stack, Mainland is the "Human Interface" layer:
- **Mainland:** Work and productivity execution.
- **Bastion:** Financial infrastructure.
- **Remotica:** Communication protocols.

## Key Modules
- **ProLab (Proposal Laboratory):** A dedicated environment for building, refining, and validating business proposals and project blueprints.
- **Workstation:** The central mission control for a "Looper" (ecosystem professional) to manage their active projects, tasks, and squads.
- **Library:** A secure, tokenized storage system for project-related files and intellectual property.
- **Academy:** A training ground for professional certifications and cognitive development within the ecosystem ruleset.

## Framework Support
Mainland is the native environment for:
- **Smart Work Cells:** Atomic units of work that trigger automated payments via Bastion when completed.
- **Dynamic SQUADs:** On-demand teams assembled with specific biomes (Systems, Marketing, Business, Leadership, etc.).
