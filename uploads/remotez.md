# RemoteZ — Lifestyle for Digital Nomads

## Overview
**RemoteZ** is the lifestyle brand within the **CodeLoop Ecosystem** that designs and provides products for the decentralized workforce. It focuses on the needs of "Loopers" who live and work globally.
