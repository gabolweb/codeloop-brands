# TCA — Transcontinental Cooperation Alliance

## Overview
**TCA (Transcontinental Cooperation Alliance)** is a strategic venture focused on large-scale international alliances and cross-continental trade cooperation. It acts as a facilitator for agreements that span multiple global jurisdictions and economic zones.

## Mission
To foster and secure high-value partnerships across continents, integrating global markets through a unified framework of cooperation and mutual growth.

## Strategic Focus
- **Global Alliances:** Building the network of partners that form the backbone of the ecosystem's international reach.
- **Cross-Continental Trade:** Facilitating the flow of value, resources, and knowledge between diverse geographic regions.
- **Unified Cooperation Framework:** Standardizing the rules and expectations for multi-party international agreements.
